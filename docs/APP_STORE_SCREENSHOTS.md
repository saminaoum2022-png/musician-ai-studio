# App Store screenshots — NabadAi Music

Capture on your **physical iPhone** (you already passed QA there).  
Apple’s required size for **6.7" display** is **1290 × 2796** — a normal iPhone screenshot from 14 Pro Max / 15 Pro Max / 16 Pro Max is correct automatically.

---

## Before you shoot

- [ ] **Signed in** as your main account (songs, Friends posts, Persona if possible)
- [ ] **Dark theme** (app default)
- [ ] **Status bar** looks clean (Wi‑Fi, battery — not on 1%)
- [ ] **Dismiss keyboard** before each shot
- [ ] **Avoid** Credits screen that says “Ask Samy — closed friends beta”
- [ ] Footer build string is tiny — OK to leave; crop later if you add marketing frames

**How to capture:** **Side button + Volume Up** → screenshot saves to Photos.

---

## Shot list (do these 6)

### 1 — Create / Generate

**Go to:** bottom tab **Create** (center) → **Lyrics** tab selected

**Show on screen:**
- A real lyric line (not empty)
- A style/genre selected
- **Generate song** button visible at bottom

**Optional caption (Canva later):** *Turn ideas into full songs*

---

### 2 — Player

**Go to:** generate a song OR open a finished track from **Profile** / mini player

**Show on screen:**
- Cover art (real, not logo placeholder)
- Track title
- Play controls / waveform
- Ideally **paused** on a nice frame (not mid-loading)

**Optional caption:** *Studio-quality tracks in minutes*

---

### 3 — Hum

**Go to:** **Create** → tap **Hum** tab

**Show on screen:**
- Mic / record UI
- “Hum a melody” vibe — record button visible

**Optional caption:** *Hum a melody — AI does the rest*

---

### 4 — Persona

**Go to:** **Profile** tab (bottom right) → scroll to **Persona** section

**Show on screen:**
- Persona tile / “Your voice” / Record or saved persona

If empty: tap **Record** so the mic UI shows (don’t need to finish recording).

**Optional caption:** *Your voice, your sound*

---

### 5 — Friends

**Go to:** **Friends** tab (bottom)

**Show on screen:**
- At least one post in the feed (your song or a friend’s)
- Likes / play UI visible

If feed empty: post one track first, then screenshot.

**Optional caption:** *Share tracks with your circle*

---

### 6 — Proof of creation (optional but strong)

**Go to:** **Profile** → your releases / a song → open **Proof** / “Created with NabadAi” certificate

**Show on screen:**
- Certificate card with cover art + track title + “Created with NabadAi”

**Optional caption:** *Verified proof you made it*

---

## After capture

1. **Photos** app → confirm 6 images, portrait, sharp text
2. **AirDrop** to Mac (or iCloud Photos on Mac)
3. Create folder: `App Store Screenshots/`
4. Rename in order:
   - `01-generate.png`
   - `02-player.png`
   - `03-hum.png`
   - `04-persona.png`
   - `05-friends.png`
   - `06-proof.png`

### Optional marketing frames

Apple accepts **plain screenshots** — frames are optional.

Free tools to add headline text:

- [Canva](https://canva.com) — “App Store Screenshot” template
- [Figma](https://figma.com) — 1290 × 2796 frame

Use captions from the table above. Keep NabadAi purple/teal gradient on text if you brand it.

---

## Upload (when App Store Connect is ready)

1. App Store Connect → your app → **1.0** → **App Store** tab
2. **Screenshots** → **6.7" Display** (iPhone 15 Pro Max)
3. Drag images **in story order** (Generate first → Proof last)
4. Apple scales these for smaller iPhones automatically in many cases; add 6.5" set only if Connect asks at submit time
