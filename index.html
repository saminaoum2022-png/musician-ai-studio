Tips for a clean scan
  1. Warm up lightly ~30s — no shouting.
  2. Glide slowly chest → head on “ah” (~6–8s).
  3. Hold your highest comfortable note ~2s.

Pick Men’s or Women’s so the printed range matches how you sing.
